Xplore is coming early this year.

Security

Xplore is powered by WebKit, a powerful, secure, and open source browser engine developed and maintained by Apple.

Performance

Experience lightning-fast browsing speeds with optimized rendering and top-notch resource management thanks to the efficiency and speed of WebKit.

Design

A sleek, modern interface gives you an effortless experience as you breeze across websites with ease. Inspired by the latest UI design trends, you'll be browsing in style. Plus, innovative features like Scrollable Tabs make Xplore perfect for uncluttered browsing and multitasking.

Features

Xplore has all the features you need, and none of the features you don't*, including word clock and a built-in ad blocker, called Vanish.

Compatibility

Works on macOS 13 (Ventura) and later. Due to Xplore's configuration, it'll always be running the WebKit version that your Mac has, so when you update your Mac, Xplore updates, too.

Environment

I'm the one and only developer of Xplore, which means that, unlike other web browsers (which have massive development teams made up of thousands of people), Xplore has a significantly smaller negative impact on the environment. Maybe I'll plant one tree for every 5 people who use Xplore!

*Some users may find the word clock feature completely unnecessary and useless.
